Help & Instructions

Quick guidance for using the Purchase Request system.

New Request

Fill in the form, select the LHIO, complete item details, and save. The request will appear in Records automatically.

Dashboard

Monitor totals, trends, and active departments. Use filters to drill into branches, months, and years.

Records

Search, view, edit, or archive active purchase requests. The Records page shows your current saved requests.

Archive

Restore a previously archived record back into active records. Archived items are kept for easy recovery.
